How to play this game

To start the game, select the level and press the button New Game.
You need to find the pairs. Click on the first card, you flip it over to show its face. Then you click on the second card.
If the cards do not match, the cards flip to face down. The game ends with a win if you find all the pairs before the time runs out.
Click on the Speaker button to turn off/on the sound effects. If you wish to see again this window, click on the button Rules.
